Once Upon a Time...

    there was a girl, called Lyn, who was told...


  • A degree by this age, a job by that
  • A house and a spouse, and two furry cats
  • A baby by then, and a retirement to plan
  • A life lived according to society's grand plan

  • Everyday, without questioning it,
  • she would wake up and accept these given beliefs.

  • One day…she realised with disbelief, that..
  • Society's timeline, a constant press
  • A never-ending stress
  • To reach milestones, on schedule and time
  • A race that's never-ending, a climb

  • Because of that… she told herself
  • “No more running, hiding, or fear
  • Just living in the moment, my dear
  • With every step, I'll pave the way.
  • For a brighter future, come what may”

  • Because of that…
  • she created a world of her own, where she is free
  • to live her life, wild and carelessly
  • Until finally…
  • a tale of triumph, and true success
  • creating a life story, that she doesn’t need to escape, is the best.
